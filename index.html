About

DevFests are large, community-run developer conferences organized around the globe with a focus on community building and learning about various Google technologies. Women Techmakers and Developer Student Clubs Manipal bring to you DevFest 2019.

Join us on the 29th of September for one day of talks and workshops on the latest Google technologies ranging from TensorFlow and Keras to Flutter and ARCore.

This year, we have two concurrent tracks and the attendees may choose to attend one of these based on their interests.

Track 1 of DevFest is aimed at providing a practical introduction to deep learning. We will kick off with a gentle introduction to Python and Deep Learning. This will be followed by live-coding sessions where we will write their own neural networks with TensorFlow and Keras. Then we will move on to learn more about GANs.

Track 2 will follow a hands-on approach to learn about Mobile App Development and Augmented Reality. The first session of this track will introduce the attendees to the basics of App Development, Material Design, and Flutter. This will be followed by a session on Augmented Reality where attendees will use Google's ARCore SDK to develop AR applications with Flutter.

Install Fest

For Track 2, to participate in the DevFest and to make the most of all the workshops, the attendees are requested to come with all requisite software pre-installed and USB cable. To help with the process we are organizing an install fest on 27th September 2019, Friday.

Delegate cards will be given to students after their installations are verified by our volunteers. The softwares required are:

    • Android Studio
    • Flutter Plugin
    • Adobe XD
    • Sublime Text

TRACK 1: Keras

Keras is a high-level neural networks API, written in Python and capable of running on top of TensorFlow, CNTK, or Theano. It was developed with a focus on enabling fast experimentation. The motivation behind using Keras is to be able to go from idea to result with the least possible delay.

TRACK 1: TensorFlow

TensorFlow is an end-to-end open source platform for machine learning. It has a comprehensive, flexible ecosystem of tools, libraries and community resources that lets researchers push the state-of-the-art in ML and developers easily build and deploy ML powered applications.

TRACK 2: Flutter

Flutter is Google’s UI toolkit for building beautiful, natively compiled applications for mobile, web, and desktop from a single codebase. It enables developers to develop expressible and flexible UIs while delivering native performance with a fast development cycle.

TRACK 2: ARCore

With ARCore, developers can build new augmented reality experiences that seamlessly blend the digital and physical worlds. Transform the way people play, shop, learn, create, and experience the world together—at Google scale.
