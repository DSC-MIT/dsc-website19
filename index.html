About

DevFests are large, community-run developer conferences organized around the globe with a focus on community building and learning about various Google technologies. Women Techmakers and Developer Student Clubs Manipal bring to you DevFest 2019.

Join us on the 29th of September for one day of talks and workshops on latest Google technologies ranging from Actions on Google to Material Design and Flutter.

This year, we have two concurrent tracks and the attendees may choose to attend one of these based on their interests.

The WTM track will kickoff with an introduction to Material Design, UI/UX and then will move on to mobile app development with Flutter.

The Developer Students' Club will walk the participants through developing Actions For Google Assistant alongside covering Firebase

Install Fest

To participate in the DevFest and to make the most of all the workshops we recommend the attendees to come with all requisite softwares pre-installed. To help with the process we are organizing an install fest on 21st September 2018. The softwares required are:

    • Android Studio
    • Flutter Plugin
    • Adobe XD
    • Sublime Text
